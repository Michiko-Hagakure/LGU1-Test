## About This Survey

This survey is designed to measure user acceptance of the LGU1 Public Facilities Reservation System based on the **Technology Acceptance Model (TAM)**. The TAM framework evaluates:

1. **Perceived Usefulness (PU)** - The degree to which a user believes the system enhances their performance
2. **Perceived Ease of Use (PEOU)** - The degree to which the system is free from effort
3. **Behavioral Intention to Use (BI)** - The user's intention to continue using the system

---

## Survey Instructions

- Please rate each statement on a scale of **1 to 5**
- **1** = Strongly Disagree
- **2** = Disagree
- **3** = Neutral
- **4** = Agree
- **5** = Strongly Agree
