## 4. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used for:

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Processing facility reservations
   - Sending booking confirmations and reminders
   - Generating official receipts

2. **Identity Verification**
   - AI-powered selfie-to-ID matching during registration
   - Preventing fraudulent accounts
   - Ensuring one account per citizen

3. **Communication**
   - Booking status updates
   - Payment confirmations
   - System announcements
   - Customer support

4. **Security & Compliance**
   - Fraud detection and prevention
   - Audit trail maintenance
   - Legal compliance

5. **Analytics (Aggregated/Anonymized)**
   - Facility utilization reports
   - Service improvement
   - Resource planning

## 7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.2+ for data in transit, encryption for sensitive data at rest
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, principle of least privilege
- **Authentication:** Password hashing (bcrypt), OTP verification, session management
- **Monitoring:** Audit trails, intrusion detection
- **Backup:** Regular encrypted backups with secure storage

## 8. Your Rights (Data Subject Rights)

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

### 8.1 Right to be Informed
- Know what data we collect and why
- Access this Privacy Policy at any time

### 8.2 Right to Access
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Request: Via profile settings or contact DPO
- Response time: Within 30 days

### 8.3 Right to Correction
- Update inaccurate or incomplete data
- Request: Via profile settings
- Response time: Immediate for self-service, 15 days for requests

### 8.4 Right to Erasure
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Limitations: Cannot delete data required for legal compliance
- Request: Contact DPO
- Response time: Within 30 days

### 8.5 Right to Object
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- Request: Contact DPO
- Response time: Within 30 days

### 8.6 Right to Data Portability
- Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
- Request: Contact DPO
- Response time: Within 30 days

### 8.7 Right to File a Complaint
- File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission
- NPC Website: https://www.privacy.gov.ph
